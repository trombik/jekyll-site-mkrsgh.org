A custom `youtube` liquid tag is available. In your post:

```
{% raw -%}
{% youtube https://www.youtube.com/embed/zpOULjyy-n8?rel=0 %}
{% endraw -%}
```

becomes:

{% youtube https://www.youtube.com/embed/zpOULjyy-n8?rel=0 %}
